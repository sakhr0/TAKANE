------------------------------------------------------TAKANE™ GAME------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Open Source - 2D - Simple build Game that refers to the french text of Takané et le photographe where The photographer visits a family which has an old woman slave known as "TAKANE" , in this game , I suppose that the photographer comes again to save TAKANE from this slavery and to reach TAKANE , he must first saves two other slaves known as Rikane and Amane along with running from the families where live the poor 3 Slaves .
